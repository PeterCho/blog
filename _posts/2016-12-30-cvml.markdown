## RL system architecture

$$ \begin{align}
Q(s_n,a_n)  \leftarrow
(1 - \alpha) Q(s_n,a_n) + \alpha \bigl [ r_n + \gamma \, {\rm max}_b
Q(s'_{n+1},b) \bigr ].  \\
  \end{align}
$$

Here $0 \le \alpha \le 1$ represents a TD learning rate, while $0 \le
\gamma \le 1$ denotes a discount factor for future rewards [9].
